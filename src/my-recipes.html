Stuffed Mushrooms

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lb mushrooms
  • 2 tbsp. butter
  • 2 tbsp. minced garlic
  • 1/4 c. breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 c. Parmesan
  • 4 oz. cream cheese
  • 2 tbsp. parsley
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit. Remove stems from mushrooms and chop them up. Place mushroom caps on baking sheet.
  2. In a skillet over medium heat melt the butter and add the chopped mushroom stems. Cook until the moisture is out (about 5 minutes.) Add garlic and let cook again for 60 seconds. Add breadcrumbs and season with salt and pepper and let cook for another 3 minutes.
  3. In a mixing bowl mix together the mushroom stem mixture, Parmesan, cream cheese, and parsley. Season again with salt and pepper. Fill the mushroom caps with filling.
  4. Bake until mushrooms are soft and the tops are golden (about 20 minutes.)

Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 lb. noodles
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 tbsp. minced garlic
  • 1 (28 oz.) can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 (16 oz.) can of tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 oz.) can of tomato paste
  • 2 tsp. dried oregano
  • salt
  • papper

Directions

  1. Combine ground beef, onion, and garlic in a large saucepan. Cook and stir until meat is brown and vegetables are tender.
  2. Stir diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste into the pan. Season with oregano, salt, and pepper. Let the spaghetti sauce simmer for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  3. Boil noodles until al dente and drain the water out. Mix your noodles with your sauce.

Sweet & Spicy Glazed Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1/4 c. brown sugar
  • 1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 tsp. paprika
  • 1 tbsp. olive oil
  • 1 1/2 lb. boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • salt

Directions

  1. In a small bowl, combine the brown sugar, cayenne pepper, garlic powder, paprika, and salt.
  2. Pat chicken thighs dry with a paper towel. Generously rub both sides of each chicken thigh.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chicken and cook until both sides of the chicken are browned and chicken is cooked through.
